| Metric        | Definition                                                           | Formula                    | Focus                         |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **Precision** | Of all items predicted as positive, how many were actually positive? | Precision = TP / (TP + FP) | Minimizes **False Positives** |
| **Recall**    | Of all actual positive items, how many were correctly identified?    | Recall = TP / (TP + FN)    | Minimizes **False Negatives** |
