## Key Learning

Today I learned that two models can have the same accuracy but make very different mistakes. As an AI engineer, I should analyze the type of mistakes a model makes, not just its overall accuracy. In real-world applications like factories, the consequences of a mistake are often more important than the accuracy percentage itself.
