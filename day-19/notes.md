Can 100% accuracy mean a perfect model?


No. accuracy 100 % does not garuntee the perfect model.
because,
100% on the training data
This often means the model has memorized the training examples.
It may be overfitting, meaning it performs perfectly on known data but poorly on new, unseen data.
Example:
Training accuracy: 100%
Test accuracy: 58%
This is not a perfect model.
